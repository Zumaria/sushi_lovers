Popular Types of Sushi for Beginners

If you're just rolling in (no pun intended) to the sushi party, it can be intimidating to know where to start - there is such a wide selection! The first thing to note is there are three main types of sushi:

Maki

Nigiri

Sashimi

While these are the main categories of sushi, they are not the only ones. Another popular style of sushi is known as the "hand roll". The hand roll is usually a single piece, and can often customized for the customer's preferences. However, for beginners, you generally start by trying one of the three styles above.


What type you start with exactly is entirely up to you! We recommend any of the following as a good first choices with mild flavors and agreeable textures.
