What is sushi?

Sushi began in atiquity, when rice and cultivation began in Japan over 2,000 years ago. Sushi was born out of the tradition of peserving fish in fermented rice. Eventually, people began to eat the rice as well as the fish - turns out, it was delicious!

In modern days, sushi typically includes the combination of various ingredients:

  1. Fish (cooked or raw)
  2. Rice
  3. Nori (seaweed)
  4. Other fillings

Sushi's roots can be traced back to Japan, it quickly began to spread to other cultures. Currently, New York City has the second best sushi after Japan. Closely followed is Bangkok, London and Paris. Taking the world by storm, sushi is gaining popularity as people become more educated about it, which is what we here at Sushi lovers is here to help you with!
